04 — The Main Event

The Rethink Tank

This is the activity that past cohorts still cannot stop talking about. Think Shark Tank, but make it product. Think real-time competition, team energy, and the kind of adrenaline you do not get from a Zoom call.

Here is how it works: you will be grouped into teams on the spot, given a real problem to crack, and asked to build a pitch from scratch. No laptops. No phones. No AI. Just pen, paper, coloured markers, whiteboards, and whatever your brain and your team can come up with. Then you pitch it live to a panel.

Do not prepare anything for this beforehand. That is the whole point — you show up, you get surprised, and that is what makes it fun. The best part is the chaos of figuring it out together in real time. More details will be shared at the retreat itself. Just come ready to have a great time.

And how you get in.

Rethink is not just a learning program. It has a consulting vertical — the Rethink Accelerator — where the team works directly with real companies to help them build, improve, and scale their products. Real companies. Real problems. Real product work. The kind of work that most people spend years trying to get access to.

When the Rethink team needs consultants for these projects, they do not post a job listing. They do not run an application process. They go to the people they know. People they have sat across from. People they have watched think on their feet. People whose instincts they trust because they have seen them in action — not on a resume, but in a room.

The retreat is that room.

Real product consulting work

Work directly with companies on live product challenges. Not case studies. Not simulations. Actual products with actual users and actual stakes.

Trust, not applications

There is no form to fill. No interview panel. The team picks people they have spent real time with and whose thinking they have seen first-hand. The retreat is the most concentrated way to build that trust.

A career accelerant

Past consultants have gone on to land PM roles at startups and product companies with the accelerator work as their strongest portfolio proof. This is not a certificate. It is real, referenceable product work.

Three days of being seen, heard, and understood by the people who make these decisions. That is what the retreat gives you.
